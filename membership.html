Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time with 30 days notice. No hidden fees or long-term contracts.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! Students get 20% off on all membership plans with a valid student ID.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect from the next billing cycle.

Is there a joining fee?

We occasionally waive the joining fee during promotional periods. Contact us for current offers.
